The concept
A concert
like nowhere
else

For the first time in Bordeaux, you decide the programme. Choose your favourite pieces, vote, and the top three are performed live by a professional string quartet.

Pop, Rock, Classical, Cinema: a repertoire that spans genres and generations, elegantly rearranged for acoustic instruments.

Concerts every 30 minutes.
